Functional Phrases Inside the Supermarket

English Expression Português
Where can I find the…? Onde posso encontrar o…?
Do you have any…? Você tem…?
I'm looking for… Estou procurando…
Can I help you? Posso te ajudar?
How much is this? Quanto custa isso?
Is this on sale? Está em promoção?
That's a good deal. Está valendo a pena / É um bom preço.
I'd like 500g of… Gostaria de 500g de…
I'll take two, please. Vou levar dois, por favor.
Can I pay by card? Posso pagar com cartão?
Paper or plastic? Sacola de papel ou plástico?
I brought my own bag. Eu trouxe minha própria sacola.
Do you have a loyalty card? Você tem o cartão fidelidade?
I forgot mine. Can I use my phone number? Esqueci o meu. Posso usar meu telefone?

Role Play Dialogue: Customer & Clerk

Customer

Excuse me, where can I find the yogurt?

Clerk

It's in the dairy section, aisle 7.

Customer

Thank you. And do you have any organic options?

Clerk

Yes, right next to the flavored ones.

Customer

Great, I'll take two. Also, do you have a loyalty card?

Clerk

Yes! Just scan it at checkout to get your discount.

Practice Tip: Try this dialogue with a partner, switching roles. Then create your own variations using different products from the vocabulary lists above.
